DATA PIPELINE

Bridging clinical MRI data to high-fidelity 3D assets for AI Research & Surgical Simulation.

01

Volumetric Segmentation

Software: 3D Slicer

Beginning with raw DICOM MRI datasets, I perform precise segmentation of intracranial pathology. This involves using thresholding and manual tracing tools to define the boundaries of the tumor (ICD-10: C71.9).

  • Multi-Volume Data Loading & Alignment
  • Segment Editor Labeling for Malignant Neoplasms
  • Smoothing & Topological Refinement
02

Advanced Data Processing

Software: ParaView

The segmented mesh is imported into ParaView for scientific rendering. I apply custom scalar opacity mapping and volume rendering techniques to visualize density variations within the tumor tissue.

  • Iso-surface Extraction & Clipping
  • Voxel-to-Mesh Optimization for AI Training
  • Custom Color Mapping based on Hounsfield Units (HU)
03

Diagnostic Synthesis

Software: Audacity | 4K Render Engine

The final 3D asset is integrated with a professional technical voiceover. This step bridges the visualization with clinical documentation (CPT 70553), creating a comprehensive multimodal training asset.

  • Technical Narration for Medical Informatics
  • Cinematic 4K Rotational Export
  • Alignment with AI Data Annotation Standards
