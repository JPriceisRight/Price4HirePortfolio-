3D Medical Visualization: Brain Tumor Analysis

Software Utilization: ParaView | 3D Slicer | Audacity

"This project demonstrates a sophisticated synthesis of Scientific Visualization and Medical Informatics. Utilizing 3D Slicer for initial volumetric segmentation of a brain MRI and ParaView for advanced data processing, I transformed raw clinical data into high-fidelity 3D digital assets. This workflow bridges the gap between diagnostic imaging and AI research-ready assets. The visualization is paired with a professional technical voiceover, providing a detailed interpretation of the pathology. This asset corresponds to clinical findings documented under ICD-10-CM Code: C71.9 (Malignant neoplasm of brain, unspecified), ensuring the visualization remains grounded in precise medical coding standards for cutting-edge AI research."
