## 🔬 Core Objective
This portfolio is a technical demonstration of **Multimodal Synthesis**. It is designed to showcase the intersection of high-fidelity video physics and cinematic audio composition, specifically tailored for evaluation by elite AI training teams.

## 🛠 Tech Stack & Methodology
* **Video Physics (Google Veo):** The "Teal Liquid Glass" background was prompted to test fluid dynamics, refraction accuracy, and temporal consistency.
* **Audio-Visual Alignment ("808" Synthesis):** A case study in synchronizing professional audio transients with generative cinematic motion.
* **Front-End Architecture:** * **Scrolledex Parallax:** Custom JavaScript to tie scroll velocity to background displacement.
* **Tailwind CSS:** Utilizing a custom dark-mode palette (`#020617`) with teal accents (`#2dd4bf`) for maximum contrast and readability.
* **AOS (Animate on Scroll):** Orchestrating entry animations for high-impact content delivery.
